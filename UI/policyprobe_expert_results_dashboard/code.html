Inadequate Encryption Disclosure

HIGH RISK

The policy mentions "industry standard encryption" but fails to specify protocol versions (e.g., TLS 1.3) or data-at-rest encryption standards (AES-256). In an educational context, this ambiguity is a critical failure of transparency.

security Action: Verify technical specs via Security Addendum

Data Retention Ambiguity

MODERATE

While inactive accounts are anonymized after 180 days, the policy does not define "inactive" status for students whose accounts are managed by district admins, potentially leading to indefinite data persistence.

Sub-processor Transparency

Audit identifies 4 sub-processors (analytics and hosting) that are not explicitly named in the public privacy policy, violating the transparency principle of modern privacy frameworks.
